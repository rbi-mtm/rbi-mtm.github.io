_Our group is announcing five master thesis topics related to atomistic modelling of materials._

<!--more-->

The available topics and supervisors are:

- Univerzalni strojno naučeni modeli za atomističke simulacije [(**Ivor Lončarić**)](/author/ivor-loncaric)
- Using machine-learned interatomic potentials to calculate excited-state properties of nano-porous graphene [(**Bernhard Kretz**)](/author/bernhard-kretz)
- Physics-informed machine learning of Hamiltonians for nano-porous graphene [(**Bernhard Kretz**)](/author/bernhard-kretz)
- Simulacije kvazi-dvodimenzionalnih hibridnih halogenidnih perovskita iz prvih principa [(**Juraj Ovčar**)](/author/juraj-ovcar)
- Simulacije visokoentropijskih slitina na bazi volframa za fuzijske primjene [(**Juraj Ovčar**)](/author/juraj-ovcar)

[**Click here**](https://www.pmf.unizg.hr/images/50005072/TEME2026.pdf) to read the abstracts of the topics and [**send us an email**](/contact/) if you want to learn more.
